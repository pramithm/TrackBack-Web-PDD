# TrackBack – AI-Powered Lost & Found Recovery Platform

## Overview

TrackBack is an AI-powered Lost & Found mobile application designed to help users recover lost belongings safely, efficiently, and securely. The platform connects individuals who have lost items with those who have found them while minimizing fraud through intelligent verification mechanisms.

Every day, valuable belongings such as mobile phones, wallets, bags, keys, documents, electronics, and pets are misplaced. Although many people are willing to return found items, there is often no reliable way to identify the rightful owner. TrackBack addresses this challenge by providing a secure, AI-driven recovery ecosystem.

Unlike traditional Lost & Found systems, TrackBack integrates Artificial Intelligence throughout the recovery process. AI validates uploaded images, generates ownership verification questions, evaluates claimant responses, matches lost and found items, and monitors user interactions to maintain platform safety.

## How TrackBack Works

### 1. Item Upload & Verification

When a user finds an item, they upload its image and details to TrackBack. AI analyzes the image and verifies that it represents a valid lost-and-found object. Inappropriate, unrelated, or unsafe content is automatically rejected.

### 2. AI Question Generation

The system generates item-specific verification questions based on the uploaded object's characteristics. These questions serve as ownership proof during the claiming process.

### 3. Ownership Verification

Users claiming an item must answer the generated verification questions. AI evaluates the responses and determines the likelihood that the claimant is the genuine owner.

### 4. Secure Communication

Once ownership confidence reaches an acceptable threshold, the finder and claimant can communicate through TrackBack's secure in-app messaging system.

### 5. Item Recovery

After successful verification and communication, the item can be safely returned to its rightful owner.
